PVE Paste Helper

Open a PVE VM console (noVNC) and you'll see a paste panel in the bottom-right corner.

Use your normal paste shortcut (Ctrl+V or ⌘V) or the panel button to type text into the VM.

Snippets

Download all saved snippets as a JSON file.

Snippets you save stay on this device only. No data is sent to any server.
